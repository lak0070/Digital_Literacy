# 🔐 Stay Safe Online Checklist (India 🇮🇳)

## ✅ Safety Tips
- 🔑 Use strong, unique passwords for every account (consider a password manager)
- 🔐 Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social apps
- 📧 Verify emails & links before clicking (check sender address and URL carefully)
- 📶 Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks; use a trusted network or VPN
- 📱 Keep your phone & apps updated to the latest versions
- 🚫 Download apps only from official stores (Play Store/App Store)
- 🧾 **UPI Safety:** Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
- 💸 **UPI Safety:** Always double-check the receiver’s name/UPI ID before sending money
- 🎓 Beware of fake internship/job offers asking for fees or personal details
- 🔗 Don’t click on suspicious “KYC update”, “lottery”, or “refund” links
- 📸 Limit sharing of personal info (ID cards, address, phone number) online
- 👥 Adjust privacy settings on social media (keep profiles restricted if needed)
- 🧠 If something feels urgent or “too good to be true”, pause and verify

---

## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong
- 📞 Call **1930** immediately for financial/cyber fraud assistance  
- 🌐 Report online at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 🏦 Inform your bank/app instantly to block transactions  
- 📄 Save evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, messages)
